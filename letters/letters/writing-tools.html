Principle: reduce friction

Your “tool stack” should make publishing easier, not more sophisticated. If it adds decisions, it is not helping.

My minimal stack

  • One place to draft
  • One place to publish
  • One place to capture ideas

What I ignore

Anything that makes me tune settings instead of writing. The best workflow is the one you can sustain.
